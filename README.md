# i am MM.

An online portfolio for Matt Manning, Junior Software Developer. The site holds capabailties to download a printable CV or testimonials document, view showcase projects and connect with Matt via a 'Lets work together form'. The goal of the site is for Matt to have an online presence and showcase some of his abilities, whilst future employers and collaborators can view his work, and get to know him and his history. For the user the site gives a concise introduction to Matt and the ability to gain copies of his documents.
